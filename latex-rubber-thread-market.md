<p><p>Latex Rubber Thread market consists of two main types: Talc Coated Rubber Thread (TCR) and Silicon Coated Rubber Thread (SCR). TCR is a type of latex rubber thread that is coated with talc powder to reduce friction and improve the thread's durability. On the other hand, SCR is a type of latex rubber thread that is coated with silicone, providing a smoother surface and better elasticity. Both TCR and SCR have their specific uses in various industries such as apparel, textiles, and medical devices.</p></p>
