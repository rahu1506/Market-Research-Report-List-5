<p><p>Semiconductor etching systems are used in the semiconductor industry for the precise removal of materials from a semiconductor substrate. Two main types of semiconductor etching systems are semiconductor dry etching systems and semiconductor wet etching systems. Semiconductor dry etching systems use plasma to remove material through chemical reactions, while semiconductor wet etching systems use liquid chemicals to dissolve material. Both systems are critical for producing high-quality semiconductor devices and are essential in the fabrication process of integrated circuits.</p></p>
