<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The HgCdTe Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LN2 Cooled</li><li>Thermoelectrically (TE) Cooled</li><li>Stirling Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HgCdTe infrared detectors are used in various applications and come in different types based on their cooling mechanisms. LN2 cooled detectors use liquid nitrogen to cool the detector, providing high sensitivity but requiring frequent refilling. Thermoelectrically cooled detectors use thermoelectric modules to maintain a stable temperature without the need for cryogenic cooling. Stirling cooled detectors utilize a Stirling cooler to achieve very low temperatures efficiently, making them ideal for high-performance applications requiring fast response times and high sensitivity.</p></p>
