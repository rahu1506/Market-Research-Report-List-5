Communication jamming disrupts enemy communication systems, preventing them from receiving or sending messages. Electronic jamming disrupts enemy electronic systems, such as GPS or navigational equipment. Other types of military jammers may include cyber jamming or drone jamming, which target specific electronic systems or technologies used by the enemy. These different types of jammers are crucial in modern warfare to gain a tactical advantage.</p></p>
