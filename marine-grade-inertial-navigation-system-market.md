<p><strong>The Marine Grade Inertial Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Gyro</li><li>Ring Laser Gyro</li><li>Fiber Optics Gyro</li><li>MEMS</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine grade inertial navigation systems are typically classified into different types based on the gyro technology used. Mechanical gyro systems rely on physical rotation mechanisms, such as spinning masses, to measure orientation. Ring laser gyro systems use lasers to detect angular velocity. Fiber optics gyro systems utilize light interference patterns. MEMS (microelectromechanical systems) rely on miniaturized sensors. Other types of systems may combine multiple technologies or use alternative methods for measuring orientation. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in marine navigation.</p></p>
