<p><p>UAV GNSS antennas are an essential component for drones to receive precise satellite signals for navigation and positioning. Helix antennas are popular for their omnidirectional reception capabilities, providing consistent signal strength in all directions. Patch antennas, on the other hand, offer high gain and directional accuracy, making them ideal for long-range communication and navigation. Both types cater to different UAV applications based on their specific requirements for signal coverage and accuracy.</p></p>
