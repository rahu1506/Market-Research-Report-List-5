<p><p>Linear Detector Array (LDA) is widely used in two main market types: Single Channel and Multi-channel. </p><p>In the Single Channel market, LDAs consist of a single row of detectors that are used for detecting and measuring one specific parameter at a time. This configuration is simpler and more cost-effective for applications that require only a single input.</p><p>On the other hand, in the Multi-channel market, LDAs consist of multiple rows of detectors that can monitor and measure multiple parameters simultaneously. This configuration is more versatile and suitable for complex applications that require monitoring of multiple inputs.</p></p>
