<p><p>Gallium Arsenide High Electron Mobility Transistors (GaAs HEMTs) are classified into two main market types: Enhanced and Depletion. Enhanced GaAs HEMTs utilize a high-bandgap AlGaAs layer to improve carrier confinement, resulting in enhanced electron mobility. On the other hand, Depletion GaAs HEMTs rely on a low-bandgap composition to induce a negative charge in the channel, enhancing device performance. Both market types cater to specific applications and offer unique advantages in terms of speed, power efficiency, and overall performance.</p></p>
