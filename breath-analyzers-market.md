<p><p>Breath analyzers are devices used to measure the alcohol content in one's breath. Semiconductor analyzers use a sensor to detect alcohol molecules. Infrared analyzers measure alcohol levels by analyzing the absorption of infrared light. Crystal analyzers use a crystal to detect alcohol molecules. Each type of breath analyzer has its own advantages and limitations.</p></p>
