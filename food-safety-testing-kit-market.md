<p><ul><li>PCR-based</li><li>Immunoassay-based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food Safety Testing Kits are divided into different types based on the testing methods used. PCR-based kits utilize polymerase chain reaction to detect specific DNA sequences of pathogens in food samples. Immunoassay-based kits detect antigens or antibodies to identify pathogens. Other types of kits may use different technologies such as biosensors or chromatography for food safety testing. Each type of kit has its own advantages and limitations, and the choice of kit depends on the specific requirements of the food safety testing process.</p></p>
