<p><p>Neurofeedback market types include Slow Cortical Potential Neurofeedback (SCP-NF), which focuses on regulating brain waves associated with attention and self-regulation. The Low-Energy Neurofeedback System (LENS) utilizes a low-intensity electromagnetic field to promote brain wave regulation. Hemoencephalographic (HEG) Neurofeedback measures blood flow in the brain to enhance cognitive function. Other types of neurofeedback include Z-score training, alpha-theta training, and peak performance training, each offering specific benefits for improving brain function and mental health.</p></p>
