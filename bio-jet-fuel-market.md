<p><p>Bio Jet Fuel market can be classified into two main types - Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil (HVO) and Fischer Tropsch (FT). HVO is derived from vegetable oils and fats through a hydrogenation process, resulting in a sustainable alternative to traditional jet fuel. On the other hand, FT fuel is produced through the Fischer-Tropsch process, which involves converting syngas derived from biomass or waste into liquid hydrocarbons. Both types of bio jet fuel offer promising solutions for reducing carbon emissions in the aviation industry.</p></p>
