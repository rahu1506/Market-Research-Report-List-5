<p><p>Stretch blow molding machines can be categorized into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines require minimal operator intervention as they are capable of performing all functions, including preform heating, stretching, and blowing, automatically. These machines are ideal for high-speed production and large-scale manufacturing plants. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of operator input for certain functions, making them suitable for smaller production runs or custom products that require more flexibility and adjustments during the manufacturing process.</p></p>
