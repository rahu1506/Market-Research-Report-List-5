<p><p>The Automatic Bakery Packaging Machine market consists of three main types of packaging: Primary Packaging, Secondary Packaging, and Tertiary Packaging. Primary Packaging involves the initial wrapping or containment of the bakery products, such as in bags or boxes. Secondary Packaging refers to the grouping of primary packages into larger units, like cases or trays. Tertiary Packaging is the final protective layer during transportation or storage, such as pallets or shrink wrapping. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient packaging of bakery products.</p></p>
