<p><strong>The Diaphragm Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD)</li><li>Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD)</li><li>Piston Diaphragm Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm pumps are categorized into different types based on their mode of operation. Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD) are powered by compressed air, making them suitable for applications where electricity is not readily available. Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD) are powered by electricity, providing a more consistent and controlled flow rate. Piston Diaphragm Pumps use a piston mechanism to displace fluid, offering higher pressure capabilities compared to other diaphragm pump types. Each type of diaphragm pump has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
