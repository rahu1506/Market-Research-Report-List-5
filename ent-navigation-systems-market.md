<p><p>ENT navigation systems utilize various technologies for accurate guidance during surgical procedures. Optical navigation systems use cameras and infrared sensors to track the position of surgical instruments in real-time. On the other hand, electromagnetic navigation systems use electromagnetic field generators and sensors to track the location of instruments. Both types of navigation systems provide precise guidance to the surgeon, improving the accuracy and efficiency of ENT surgeries. These navigation systems are essential tools in modern ENT surgery, allowing for safer and more successful procedures.</p></p>
