<p><p>A Vacuum Image Intensifier Tube is a technology used in night vision devices to enhance low-light images. It works by using a photocathode to convert incoming photons into electrons, which are then amplified and converted back into visible light. This technology allows for improved visibility in low-light conditions, making it a crucial component in applications such as military night vision goggles, thermal imaging cameras, and scientific instruments.</p><p>The Vacuum Image Intensifier Tube Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.40% during the forecast period.
