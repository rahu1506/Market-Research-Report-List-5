<p><p>An eddy current sensor is a non-contact device used for measuring the distance, position, or speed of conductive objects. It works based on electromagnetic induction, where a coil generates an alternating magnetic field that interacts with the conductive material, inducing eddy currents which in turn create a secondary magnetic field. By monitoring changes in the secondary magnetic field, the sensor can accurately determine various properties of the object being measured.</p><p>The Eddy Current Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.50% during the forecast period. The market growth is being driven by the increasing adoption of these sensors in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, manufacturing, and electronics. Eddy current sensors offer advantages such as high accuracy, reliability, and durability, making them ideal for applications where traditional contact sensors may not be suitable.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Eddy Current Sensor Market is the development of advanced sensors with enhanced features such as wireless connectivity, higher precision, and improved sensitivity.
