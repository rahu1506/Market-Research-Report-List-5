<p><p>FW Bell Probes are specialized instruments used for measuring magnetic fields in various applications. The market offers two primary types: axial probes and transverse probes. Axial probes measure magnetic fields along the direction of the field lines, providing accurate data for linear applications. In contrast, transverse probes measure magnetic fields perpendicular to the field lines, making them suitable for assessing transverse magnetic characteristics. Both types are essential for industrial, research, and quality control applications, ensuring precise magnetic field analysis.</p></p>
