<p><p>EDM oils or fluids are specially formulated fluids used in the electrical discharge machining (EDM) process. These fluids help to cool and lubricate the workpiece and electrode, prevent corrosion, and flush away debris during the machining process. The use of EDM oils or fluids ensures efficient and precise machining, leading to improved productivity and surface finish quality.</p><p>The EDM oils or fluids market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for precision machining in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics.
