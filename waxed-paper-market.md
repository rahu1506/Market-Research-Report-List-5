Natural-based wax paper is made from materials like soybean wax or beeswax, offering a more eco-friendly and biodegradable option. On the other hand, mineral-based wax paper is made with petroleum-based waxes, providing a more cost-effective and durable option for certain applications. Both types have their own unique benefits and are used in various industries for packaging and wrapping purposes.</p></p>
