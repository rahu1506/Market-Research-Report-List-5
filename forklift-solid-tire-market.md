<p><strong>The Forklift Solid Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cured-On Solid Tire</li><li>Pressed-On Solid Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Forklift Solid Tire market is primarily divided into two types: Cured-On Solid Tires and Pressed-On Solid Tires. Cured-On Solid Tires are made by curing rubber compounds onto a tire casing, providing enhanced durability and resistance to punctures. They are ideal for heavy-duty applications and offer better shock absorption. In contrast, Pressed-On Solid Tires are manufactured by pressing solid rubber onto a steel rim, resulting in a more rigid structure. These tires are typically used for lighter loads and provide good stability and traction.</p></p>
