<p><p>The jewelry grade lab grown diamonds market includes two main types: CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) and HPHT (High Pressure High Temperature). CVD diamonds are grown through a process where a carbon-rich gas is heated to form a diamond on a substrate. HPHT diamonds are created by subjecting a diamond seed to high pressure and high temperature to mimic natural diamond formation. Both types are increasingly popular in the jewelry industry for their high quality and ethical sourcing.</p></p>
