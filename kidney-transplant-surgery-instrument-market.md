<p><p>The kidney transplant surgery instrument market includes various types of instruments such as tissue forceps, atraumatic vascular clamps, buffer vein retractor, bulldog clamps, and others. Tissue forceps are used to hold and manipulate tissues during the surgery. Atraumatic vascular clamps are used to control blood vessels without causing damage. Buffer vein retractors are utilized to expose and access the kidney vein. Bulldog clamps are used for temporary occlusion of blood vessels. Other instruments in the market cater to specific needs during kidney transplant surgeries.</p></p>
