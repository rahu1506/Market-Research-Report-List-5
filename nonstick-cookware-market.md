<p><ul><li>PTFE-based Nonstick Cookware</li><li>Ceramic-based Nonstick Cookware</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nonstick cookware market features two primary types: PTFE-based and ceramic-based nonstick cookware. PTFE-based options, commonly known as Teflon, offer excellent food release and easy cleaning but can be sensitive to high temperatures. In contrast, ceramic-based cookware is often touted as a safer alternative, providing a natural, chemical-free nonstick surface that withstands higher heat but may not be as durable over time. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences for convenience and safety in cooking.</p></p>
