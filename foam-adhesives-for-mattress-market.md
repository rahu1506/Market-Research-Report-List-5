<p><p>Foam adhesives for mattress market includes water-based, solvent-based, and hotmelt adhesives. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and have low volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Each type has its own advantages and applications in the mattress industry. It is important for manufacturers to choose the right type of foam adhesive based on their specific requirements for bonding strength, durability, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
