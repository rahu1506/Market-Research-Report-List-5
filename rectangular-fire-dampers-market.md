Manual fire dampers are operated manually to close off airflow in the event of a fire, while motorized fire dampers are equipped with a motor that automatically closes the damper when triggered by a fire alarm system. Both types are essential in preventing the spread of smoke and fire in buildings, ensuring the safety of occupants and minimizing property damage.</p></p>
