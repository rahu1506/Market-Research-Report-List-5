<p><ul><li>Etching</li><li>Electroforming</li><li>Multi Material Composite Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fine Metal Mask (FMM) market includes three main types - Etching, Electroforming, and Multi Material Composite Method. </p><p>Etching method involves using chemicals to selectively remove metal to create the desired pattern. </p><p>Electroforming method involves depositing metal onto a mold through electroplating to create the mask.</p><p>Multi Material Composite Method combines different materials to form the mask. Each type caters to specific market demands and applications, offering different benefits in terms of precision, durability, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
