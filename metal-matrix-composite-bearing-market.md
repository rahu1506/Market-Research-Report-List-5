<p><p>Metal matrix composite bearings are commonly categorized into three types based on their applications: radial bearings, thrust bearings, and others. Radial bearings are designed to support radial loads, which are perpendicular to the axis of rotation. Thrust bearings, on the other hand, support axial loads, which are parallel to the axis of rotation. The "others" category includes specialized bearings that are designed for specific applications such as oscillating or linear motion. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various industries depending on the specific requirements.</p></p>
