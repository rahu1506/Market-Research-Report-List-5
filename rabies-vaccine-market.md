<p><p>The rabies vaccine market serves two main applications: pre-exposure prophylaxis and post-exposure prophylaxis. Pre-exposure prophylaxis involves administering the vaccine to individuals at high risk of exposure to rabies, such as veterinarians and travelers to high-risk areas. Post-exposure prophylaxis entails administering the vaccine to individuals who have been exposed to the rabies virus. Both applications aim to prevent the development of rabies disease in individuals who may have been exposed to the virus.</p></p>
