<p><p>Small excavators are typically categorized into three main types based on their operating weight. Machines with a weight of up to 6 tons are classified as mini excavators, suitable for small-scale projects and confined spaces. Excavators weighing between 6 and 13 tons fall into the compact excavator category, offering a balance between power and maneuverability. Lastly, machines weighing over 13 tons are considered standard excavators, ideal for heavy-duty tasks and larger construction projects. Each type caters to different project requirements and operational capacities.</p></p>
