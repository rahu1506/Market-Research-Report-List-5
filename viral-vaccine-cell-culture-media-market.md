<p><ul><li>Adherent</li><li>Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Viral vaccine cell culture media can be classified into two types based on cell growth characteristics: adherent and suspension markets. Adherent culture media are used for cells that require attachment to a solid surface for growth, while suspension culture media are designed for cells that grow independently in a liquid medium. Adherent culture media are ideal for cells like Vero and MRC-5, while suspension culture media are suitable for CHO and HEK293 cells. Both types play critical roles in the production of viral vaccines.</p></p>
