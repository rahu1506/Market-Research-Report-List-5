<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Delay Line Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Acoustic Wave</li><li>Bulk Acoustic Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acoustic Wave Delay Line Market is divided into two main types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW). SAW technology uses acoustic waves to transmit signals on the surface of a piezoelectric material, allowing for high-frequency applications. In contrast, BAW technology uses acoustic waves to propagate through a solid material, enabling lower frequency and higher power applications. Both types of technologies are commonly used in telecommunications, aerospace, and military applications for signal processing and filtering purposes.</p></p>
