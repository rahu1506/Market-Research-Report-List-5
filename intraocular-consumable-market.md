<p><p>The intraocular consumable market consists of different types of products such as intraocular lenses, eye silicone oil, viscoelastic solutions, artificial vitreous, and other related products. Viscoelastic solutions help maintain space in the eye during surgery. Artificial vitreous is used as a substitute for the natural vitreous humor in the eye. Other products in this market include surgical instruments and accessories.</p></p>
