<p><p>3D Volumetric Displays market is comprised of two main types: Swept-Volume Display and Solid-Volume Display. Swept-Volume Displays create images by rapidly moving a light source or creating a display volume through persistence of vision. This type of display is commonly used in holographic technology. On the other hand, Solid-Volume Displays use a physical medium, such as a fog or mist, to create 3D images. Solid-Volume Displays are often utilized in entertainment venues and theme parks to create captivating visual experiences.</p></p>
