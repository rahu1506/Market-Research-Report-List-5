<p><strong>The 2 Piece Aluminum Can Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DRD (Draw and Redraw) Cans</li><li>DWI (Drawn and Wall Ironed) Cans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 2 piece aluminum can market consists of two main types: DRD (Draw and Redraw) cans and DWI (Drawn and Wall Ironed) cans. DRD cans are made by drawing a flat disc of aluminum into a cup shape, redrawing it to the desired height, and then trimming the excess material. DWI cans, on the other hand, are produced by drawing a cup from a flat disc of aluminum and then ironing the walls to achieve the desired height. Both types of cans are widely used in the beverage packaging industry.</p></p>
