<p><p>HVAC market types can be classified into two main categories: Direct Expansion Systems and Central Air Conditioning Systems. Direct Expansion Systems include equipment like window air conditioners and mini-split units that cool air directly at the point of use. Central Air Conditioning Systems, on the other hand, use ductwork to distribute cooled air throughout a building. Both types of systems are popular choices for residential and commercial applications, offering efficient and effective cooling solutions for different needs and preferences.</p></p>
