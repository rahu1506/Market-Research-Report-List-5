<p><p>Spray-applied Fire Resistive Materials (SFRM) are specialized coatings designed to protect structural components of buildings from fire damage. These materials, often composed of cellulose, fiberglass, and cementitious formulations, are applied to surfaces like steel beams and columns to enhance the fire resistance rating of structures. The increasing implementation of stringent fire safety regulations and a growing emphasis on improving building safety standards are fueling the demand for SFRM in various sectors, including commercial, residential, and industrial construction.</p><p>Market growth analysis indicates that the SFRM market is poised for significant expansion, with projections suggesting a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.90% during the forecast period. Moreover, the rise in urbanization and infrastructure development projects around the globe are further contributing to market growth, as they necessitate effective fire protection solutions. Overall, the SFRM market is experiencing a robust upward trajectory, driven by regulatory support and innovation in fireproofing materials.</p></p>
