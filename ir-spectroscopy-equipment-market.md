<p><p>IR Spectroscopy Equipment Market includes three main types:</p><p>1. Fourier Transform Infrared Spectrometer: This type of spectrometer uses a Michelson interferometer to collect and analyze IR spectra. It offers high sensitivity and rapid scanning capabilities, making it suitable for a wide range of applications.</p><p>2. Near-Infrared Spectrometer: This type of spectrometer is used for quick and non-destructive analysis of samples. It is particularly useful in industries such as agriculture, food, and pharmaceuticals for quality control and process monitoring.</p><p>3. Others: This category includes various specialized IR spectrometers such as dispersive infrared spectrometers, far-infrared spectrometers, and microscopy-based infrared imaging systems. These spectrometers cater to specific research and industrial needs.</p></p>
