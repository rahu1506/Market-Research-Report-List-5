<p><ul><li>Chopped Carbon Fiber</li><li>Continuous Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Closed molding carbon fiber market consists of two main types: chopped carbon fiber and continuous carbon fiber. Chopped carbon fiber is made up of short, randomly oriented carbon fiber strands that are mixed with a resin and molded into a shape. Continuous carbon fiber, on the other hand, consists of long, aligned carbon fiber strands that are carefully woven into a fabric and impregnated with resin before being molded. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various industries for lightweight, high-strength applications.</p></p>
