<p><p>Belt drive fans are available in two main types - centrifugal and axial. Centrifugal fans are designed to move air or other gases in a radial direction away from the fan, creating a high-pressure system. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air in a linear direction parallel to the fan blade rotation. Both types are commonly used in various industrial and commercial applications such as HVAC systems, cooling towers, ventilation systems, and more. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
