<p><p>Halogen heaters come in various types to suit different heating needs. Oscillating heaters are designed to distribute heat evenly across a room by rotating back and forth, covering a larger area. Blower type heaters use a fan to blow warm air into the room quickly, making them ideal for providing instant heat. Both types offer efficient heating solutions, with oscillating heaters being suitable for larger spaces while blower type heaters are great for quick heating in smaller areas.</p></p>
