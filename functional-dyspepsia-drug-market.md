<p><p>Functional Dyspepsia Drug Market includes two main types of drugs - Prokinetic Drugs and Gastric Antisecretory Drugs. Prokinetic Drugs help to improve gastrointestinal motility and speed up the movement of food from the stomach to the intestines. On the other hand, Gastric Antisecretory Drugs work to reduce the production of stomach acid, which can help alleviate symptoms such as pain, bloating, and nausea. Both types of drugs are commonly used in the treatment of functional dyspepsia to help manage symptoms and improve the overall quality of life for patients.</p></p>
