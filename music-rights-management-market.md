<p><p>Music Rights Management includes various market types such as Print, Public Performance, and Others. Print rights refer to the permission needed to physically reproduce or print sheet music or lyrics. Public Performance rights involve the licensing required for music to be played in public spaces or events. Other rights can encompass digital streaming, synchronization (use of music in media), and mechanical (distribution of recordings). Each market type plays a crucial role in ensuring that music creators are properly compensated for their work.</p></p>
