They are classified into two types based on the membrane used: Low Flux Membrane and High Flux Membrane. </p><p>Low Flux Membrane dialyzers have a lower permeability, suitable for standard dialysis procedures. High Flux Membrane dialyzers have a higher permeability, allowing for better clearance of larger molecules and toxins. Both types are essential in providing effective treatment for patients with kidney failure, but their usage may vary depending on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
