<p><p>Intelligent Flow Meter Market Types include Coriolis, Magnetic, Ultrasonic, Multiphase, Vortex, Variable Area, Differential Pressure, Thermal, Turbine. Coriolis meters measure flow based on the Coriolis effect, while Magnetic meters use magnetic fields to measure flow. Ultrasonic meters use sound waves, Multiphase meters handle multiple phases of fluid, Vortex meters measure flow by creating vortices, Variable Area meters use a float in a tapered tube, Differential Pressure meters measure flow by comparing two pressure points, Thermal meters measure flow by measuring temperature change, Turbine meters measure flow by counting rotations of a turbine.</p></p>
