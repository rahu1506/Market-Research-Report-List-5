<p><p>Location-Based Services (LBS) can be categorized into Outdoor LBS and Indoor LBS markets. Outdoor LBS primarily focus on providing location-based information and services outside buildings, such as navigation, local searches, and location-based advertising. On the other hand, Indoor LBS cater to providing similar services but within buildings, such as shopping malls, airports, and hospitals. Both markets utilize technologies like GPS, Bluetooth, WiFi, and beacon technology to deliver personalized and relevant location-based information to users.</p></p>
