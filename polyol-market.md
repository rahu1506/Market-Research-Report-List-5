<p><ul><li>Polyester Polyol</li><li>Polyether Polyol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyol market can be broadly classified into two types: Polyester Polyol and Polyether Polyol. Polyester Polyol is made by the reaction of organic acids with glycols, while Polyether Polyol is produced by the reaction of epoxides with polyhydric alcohols. Polyester Polyol offers high tensile strength, while Polyether Polyol provides superior flexibility. Both types of polyols are widely used in the production of polyurethane foams, adhesives, coatings, and sealants due to their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
