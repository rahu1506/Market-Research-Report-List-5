<p><ul><li>Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS)</li><li>Modular CMTS (M-CMTS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMTS stands for Cable Modem Termination System, which is a piece of equipment used by cable providers to deliver high-speed internet access. There are two main types of CMTS on the market: Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS) and Modular CMTS (M-CMTS). I-CMTS combines the functions of a CMTS and a downstream cable modem termination system in a single chassis, while M-CMTS separates these functions into different modules for increased flexibility and scalability. Both types cater to different network deployment scenarios and offer unique advantages for cable providers.</p></p>
