<p><p>Soil conditioners help improve soil structure, enhance nutrient retention, and promote overall plant growth. Natural soil conditioners, such as compost, manure, and peat moss, are derived from organic sources and are environmentally friendly. Synthetic soil conditioners are manufactured products that are designed to mimic the effects of natural conditioners, such as gypsum and vermiculite. While both types of conditioners can be effective in improving soil quality, natural conditioners are often preferred for their sustainable and eco-friendly qualities.</p></p>
