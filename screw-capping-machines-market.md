<p><p>Screw capping machines come in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual machines require the operator to place the cap on the bottle by hand before it is tightened by the machine. Semi-automatic machines can place the cap on the bottle automatically but require manual intervention to start and stop the capping process. Automatic machines are fully automated and can cap bottles continuously without any manual assistance. Each type offers different levels of efficiency and convenience for capping operations.</p></p>
