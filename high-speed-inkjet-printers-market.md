<p><p>High speed inkjet printers are commonly categorized into two main types: continuous inkjet printers and drop-on-demand inkjet printers. Continuous inkjet printers continuously emit tiny droplets of ink onto the printing surface, allowing for fast and consistent printing. Drop-on-demand inkjet printers only release ink droplets when needed, resulting in more precise and efficient printing. Both types of printers are popular in various industries for their speed, quality, and versatility in printing a wide range of materials.</p></p>
