<p><ul><li>Vision Sensor/Camera</li><li>EPAS Actuator</li><li>Electronic Control Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lane Keep Assist System (LKAS) market is categorized into three main types: Vision Sensor/Camera, EPAS Actuator, and Electronic Control Unit (ECU). Vision Sensors/Cameras capture lane markings and road conditions, enabling the system to assess vehicle positioning. EPAS Actuators provide steering assistance to maintain lane discipline by subtly adjusting the steering wheel. The ECU processes data from sensors and actuators to execute lane-keeping interventions, ensuring safety and enhancing driving comfort in modern vehicles.</p></p>
