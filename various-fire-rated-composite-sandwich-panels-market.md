<p><p>Fire rated composite sandwich panels are categorized based on their fire resistance ratings, which indicate their performance in fire exposure. A2 fire rated panels are non-combustible and highly resistant to fire, ideal for high-risk environments. B fire rated panels offer moderate fire resistance, suitable for various commercial applications. C, D, and E ratings denote decreasing levels of fire resistance, with C being suitable for certain industrial uses, while D and E are often utilized in less critical environments. F-rated panels are less fire resistant, generally providing basic protection.</p></p>
