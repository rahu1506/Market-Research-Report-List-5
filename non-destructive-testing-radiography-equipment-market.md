<p><p>Non-destructive testing radiography equipment is used for inspecting materials and components without causing damage. The three main types of radiography equipment are X-ray, Gamma-ray, and Neutron-ray. X-ray machines use electromagnetic radiation to create images of the object's internal structure. Gamma-ray equipment uses radioactive isotopes to penetrate thick materials and provide detailed images. Neutron-ray equipment uses neutron radiation to detect defects in dense materials like steel. Each type of equipment has its own advantages and applications in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
