<p><p>Engine management sensors are essential components in modern vehicles that help optimize engine performance and fuel efficiency. Camshaft and crankshaft position sensors monitor the position of engine valves and pistons, while oxygen sensors measure oxygen levels in the exhaust to ensure proper fuel combustion. Manifold absolute pressure sensors and mass air flow sensors regulate air intake, while coolant temperature sensors prevent engine overheating. Knock sensors detect premature combustion, preventing engine damage. Overall, these sensors work together to ensure smooth and efficient engine operation.</p></p>
