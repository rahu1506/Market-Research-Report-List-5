<p><strong>The Semiconductor MEMS Probe Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical MEMS Probe Cards</li><li>Cantilever MEMS Probe Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor MEMS probe cards market encompasses two primary types: vertical MEMS probe cards and cantilever MEMS probe cards. Vertical MEMS probe cards are designed for high-density probing, facilitating efficient electrical connections in compact layouts. They enable precise testing of chips with reduced footprint. In contrast, cantilever MEMS probe cards utilize a lever-like design, providing excellent mechanical stability and flexibility. This configuration is ideal for varying test requirements and helps optimize contact with different semiconductor surface types during testing processes.</p></p>
