<p><strong>The Rolling Mill Gearboxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage Gearboxes</li><li>Double Stage Gearboxes</li><li>Multiple Stage Gearboxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rolling mill gearboxes are categorized based on the number of stages they consist of. Single stage gearboxes have just one stage of gears, offering simplicity and efficiency for less complex applications. Double stage gearboxes have two stages, providing higher torque and speed reduction capabilities for more demanding operations. Multiple stage gearboxes consist of three or more stages, delivering even greater torque and speed reduction for heavy-duty applications. Each type of gearbox offers varying levels of performance and suitability for different industrial requirements.</p></p>
