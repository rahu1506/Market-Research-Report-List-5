<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Uncooled Thermal Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microbolometer IR Detector</li><li>Thermopile IR Detector</li><li>Pyroelectric IR Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled thermal infrared detector market includes three main types of detectors: microbolometer IR detectors, thermopile IR detectors, and pyroelectric IR detectors. Microbolometer IR detectors operate by measuring changes in electrical resistance as the temperature of the detector changes. Thermopile IR detectors use multiple thermocouples to measure temperature differences. Pyroelectric IR detectors generate a voltage in response to changes in IR radiation. Each type of detector serves a specific purpose and has unique characteristics that make them suitable for different applications in the thermal imaging industry.</p></p>
