<p><p>Leaded resistors are electronic components used to limit the flow of electric current in a circuit. There are several types of leaded resistors available in the market including carbon film resistors, metal film resistors, and metal oxide resistors. Carbon film resistors are made of a carbon film deposited on a ceramic rod, while metal film resistors have a thin metal film on a ceramic base. Metal oxide resistors are made of a metal oxide film on a ceramic base. Each type of resistor has its own unique characteristics and uses in electronic circuits.</p></p>
