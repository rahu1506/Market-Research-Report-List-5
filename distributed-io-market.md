<p><p>Distributed I/O market consists of two types: Analog and Digital. Analog I/O includes sensors that measure continuous signals like voltage, current, or temperature, while Digital I/O involves binary signals that represent on/off or high/low states. Analog I/O is used for precise measurements and control in applications like temperature monitoring, while Digital I/O is commonly used for simple on/off control tasks such as turning devices on or off. Both types play a crucial role in distributed control systems across various industries.</p></p>
