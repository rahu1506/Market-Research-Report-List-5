<p><p>Wound closure products can be categorized into primary and secondary closure products. Secondary closure products are used for wounds that cannot be closed by bringing the edges together, such as dressings, skin substitutes, and negative pressure wound therapy. Both types of products play a crucial role in promoting wound healing and preventing infection.</p></p>
