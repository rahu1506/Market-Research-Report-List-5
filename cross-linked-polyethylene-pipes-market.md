<p><ul><li>Peroxide Cross-Linked Pipe</li><li>Silane Cross-Linked Pipe</li><li>Irradiation Cross-Linked Pipe</li><li>Azo Cross-Linked Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cross-Linked Polyethylene Pipes come in several types, such as Peroxide Cross-Linked Pipe, Silane Cross-Linked Pipe, Irradiation Cross-Linked Pipe, and Azo Cross-Linked Pipe. Peroxide Cross-Linked Pipe involves using peroxide to create chemical cross-links in the polyethylene. Silane Cross-Linked Pipe uses silane compounds to achieve cross-linking. Irradiation Cross-Linked Pipe involves exposing the polyethylene to radiation to induce cross-links. Azo Cross-Linked Pipe utilizes azo compounds to create cross-links. Each type of cross-linking method offers unique properties and benefits for various applications.</p></p>
