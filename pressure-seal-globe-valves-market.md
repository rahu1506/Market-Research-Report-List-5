<p><p>Pressure seal globe valves are commonly available in two market types based on their connection methods: socket weld connections and butt weld connections. Socket weld connections involve welding the valve to the pipe using a socket weld, providing a strong and leak-proof connection. Butt weld connections involve welding the valve directly to the pipe with a butt weld, also ensuring a secure and tight seal. Both types of connections are commonly used in high-pressure and high-temperature applications where reliability and safety are crucial.</p></p>
