<p><p>MEMS Ink Jet Print Head market is dominated by two main types - Thermal Print Head and Piezo Print Head. Thermal Print Heads use heat to create small vapor bubbles that propel the ink onto the paper, while Piezo Print Heads use electric currents to create pressure waves, causing the ink to be ejected onto the paper. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in different industries such as graphic arts, textiles, and packaging. The choice between the two types depends on factors like printing speed, quality, and cost.</p></p>
