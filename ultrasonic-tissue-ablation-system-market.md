<p><p>Ultrasonic Tissue Ablation Systems are advanced medical devices used for non-invasive surgeries. The two main types of these systems are High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) and Magnetic Resonance-guided Focused Ultrasound Surgery (MRgFUS). HIFU uses focused ultrasound waves to heat and ablate targeted tissue, while MRgFUS combines ultrasound imaging with magnetic resonance guidance for precise and controlled tissue ablation. Both technologies offer minimally invasive treatment options for various medical conditions, including tumors and benign growths.</p></p>
