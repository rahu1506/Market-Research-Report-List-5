<p><p>On-site milling refers to the process of milling materials directly at the location where they are needed. There are two main types of on-site milling markets: linear and gantry milling, and orbital milling. Linear and gantry milling involve using machines that move along a straight path or on multiple axes to remove material and create precise shapes. Orbital milling, on the other hand, involves a cutting tool rotating around a fixed point to achieve accurate cuts and finishes on large, complex components.</p></p>
