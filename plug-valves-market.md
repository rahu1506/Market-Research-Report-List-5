<p><p>Plug valves are available in various types to cater to different industry needs. Non-lubricated plug valves are self-lubricating, reducing maintenance requirements. Lubricated plug valves have enhanced sealing capabilities due to the lubrication. Eccentric plug valves have an offset plug design for precise throttling applications. Expanding plug valves have tapered or conical plugs that expand to seal the flow passage when closed. Each type of plug valve offers unique benefits and applications in industries such as oil & gas, chemical, and water treatment.</p></p>
