<p><p>Sputtering equipment is used in various industries such as physics, electronics, etching, and chemical applications. In physics sputtering, target material is bombarded with energetic particles to release atoms for thin film deposition. Electronic sputtering involves using high-energy ions to create thin films for electronic devices. Etching sputtering is used to remove material from a surface, while chemical sputtering involves chemically reactive gases in the sputtering process. Each type of sputtering market caters to specific industries and applications.</p></p>
