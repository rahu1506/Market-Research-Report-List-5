<p><p>Handheld Explosives Trace Detector Market Types include Mass Spectrometry Technology and Ion Mobility Spectrometry Technology. Mass Spectrometry Technology is based on the analysis of mass-to-charge ratios of ionized molecules, providing highly accurate detection capabilities. Ion Mobility Spectrometry Technology relies on the separation of ions based on their mobility in a carrier gas, allowing for rapid and sensitive detection of explosives. Both technologies are used in handheld devices for security purposes, such as at airports, border crossings, and high-profile events.</p></p>
