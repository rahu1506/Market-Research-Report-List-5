There are two main types of color vision sensors - RGB light sensors and XYZ light sensors. RGB light sensors detect red, green, and blue light to determine color, while XYZ light sensors measure the intensity of light across the entire visible spectrum. Both types of sensors are essential for ensuring accurate color detection and consistency in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
