<p><p>Marine VHF Radio market is primarily divided into two main types: Fixed-Mount and Handheld. Fixed-mount radios are installed permanently on a boat and have a more powerful transmitting range, ideal for larger vessels or use in a fixed location. Handheld radios are portable and compact, making them convenient for use on smaller boats or for emergency situations. Both types offer essential communication capabilities for marine activities, such as contacting other vessels, emergency services, and obtaining weather updates.</p></p>
