Branch/Feeder AFCI protects against arc-faults in specified branch circuits, while CAFCI protects both branch circuits and feeders within a panel. Other market types include AFCI receptacles designed for specific applications, such as bedrooms or kitchens. These options allow for customized protection against dangerous electrical faults, providing enhanced safety in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
