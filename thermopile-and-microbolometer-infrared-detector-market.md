<p><p>The Thermopile and Microbolometer infrared detector market includes two primary types of devices used for thermal imaging and temperature measurement. Microbolometer IR detectors utilize a matrix of microfabricated sensors to detect infrared radiation and convert it into an electrical signal, offering high spatial resolution. In contrast, thermopile IR detectors function by measuring the temperature difference between thermal energy absorbed and a reference heat sink, providing accurate temperature readings. Both technologies find applications in surveillance, automotive, medical diagnostics, and building safety.</p></p>
