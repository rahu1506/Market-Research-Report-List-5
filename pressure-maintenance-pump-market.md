<p><p>Pressure-maintenance pumps are crucial for maintaining pressure in pipelines or systems to ensure smooth and efficient operations. Two common types in the market are centrifugal pumps and turbine pumps. Centrifugal pumps work by using a rotating impeller to create a centrifugal force which increases the fluid pressure. Turbine pumps, on the other hand, use impellers and diffusers to generate pressure. Both types are widely used in industries such as water treatment, oil & gas, and manufacturing for maintaining stable pressure levels in systems.</p></p>
