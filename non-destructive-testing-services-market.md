<p><p>Non Destructive Testing (NDT) Services Market consists of various types such as Ultrasonic Testing, which uses high-frequency sound waves to detect flaws or defects in materials. Radiography Testing involves the use of X-rays or gamma rays to examine the internal structure of an object. Magnetic Particle Testing uses magnetic fields to identify surface and near-surface defects. Liquid Penetrant Testing involves applying a liquid to the surface of a material to detect surface-breaking defects. Other markets in this industry may include eddy current testing, visual testing, and thermal testing.</p></p>
