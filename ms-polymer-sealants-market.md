<p><p>MS Polymer Sealants are available in two main market types: One Component and Two Component. One Component sealants come ready to use in a single package, eliminating the need for mixing or measuring additional components. On the other hand, Two Component sealants require mixing of two separate components prior to application, allowing for more precise control over curing time and flexibility.
