<p><ul><li>Medium Frequency (MF) Non-directional Beacon</li><li>Low Frequency (LF) Non-directional Beacon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-directional beacon (NDB) market includes Medium Frequency (MF) NDB and Low Frequency (LF) NDB. MF NDB operates in the frequency range of 300-3000 kHz and is commonly used for aviation navigation due to its longer range and better coverage. LF NDB operates in the frequency range of 30-300 kHz and is mainly used for marine navigation. Both types of NDBs provide omnidirectional signals to help guide aircraft and ships during their journeys, making them crucial components of the navigation infrastructure.</p></p>
