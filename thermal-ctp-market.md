<p><strong>The Thermal CTP Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic CTP</li><li>Fully automatic CTP</li><li>Manual CTP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal CTP market types include semi-automatic CTP, fully automatic CTP, and manual CTP. Semi-automatic CTP systems require some manual operation, such as plate loading. Fully automatic CTP systems are more advanced, with automated plate loading and processing. Manual CTP systems involve entirely manual plate loading and processing. Each type serves different needs and budgets, with semi-automatic and manual CTP systems being more cost-effective options for smaller print shops, while fully automatic CTP systems offer efficiency and productivity for larger printing operations.</p></p>
