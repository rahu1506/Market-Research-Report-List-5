<p><p>Bottom Anti-Reflection Coatings (BARC) are essential in semiconductor manufacturing to enhance photolithography processes by reducing reflections from silicon substrates. The BARC market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic. Organic BARCs are carbon-based materials that offer flexibility and improved coating uniformity, ideal for advanced process nodes. Inorganic BARCs, typically derived from metallic oxides, provide superior stability and performance under extreme conditions. Both types are crucial for optimizing image resolution and minimizing standing waves during exposure in photolithography.</p></p>
