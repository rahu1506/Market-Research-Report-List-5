<p><p>Thermoset fillers such as oxides, silicates, and hydroxides are commonly used in various industries for their unique properties. Oxides, such as titanium dioxide, are known for their ability to improve the strength and durability of thermoset materials. Silicates, like aluminum silicate, are valued for their heat resistance and electrical insulation properties. Hydroxides, such as magnesium hydroxide, are used for flame retardancy and smoke suppression in thermoset applications. Each type of filler offers different advantages and can be tailored to specific industry needs.</p></p>
