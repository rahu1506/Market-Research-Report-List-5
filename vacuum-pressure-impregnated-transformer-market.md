<p><p>A Vacuum Pressure Impregnated Transformer (VPIT) is a type of electrical transformer that utilizes a vacuum process to enhance the insulation properties and durability of the components. This technique involves impregnating the winding insulation materials with thermosetting resin under vacuum conditions, ensuring optimal adhesion and minimized voids. The result is a transformer with improved thermal performance, reduced noise levels, and increased resistance to environmental factors.</p><p>The Vacuum Pressure Impregnated Transformer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.30% during the forecast period.
