<p><strong>The Semiconductor Bonding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire Bonder</li><li>Die Bonder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor bonding equipment market primarily encompasses wire bonders and die bonders. Wire bonders are used to create electrical connections between semiconductor chips and their packaging using fine wires, ensuring reliable signal transmission. Die bonders, on the other hand, focus on attaching semiconductor dies to substrates or lead frames, making them integral for device assembly. Both types of equipment are essential for the production of integrated circuits, contributing to the advancement of electronic devices and overall semiconductor manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
