<p><p>Direct Current Solid State Relays have various types based on their mounting options. Panel Mount relays are installed on the front panel of control panels or devices, making them easily accessible for maintenance. Din Rail Mount relays are specifically designed to be mounted on standard DIN rails, commonly used in industrial control applications for organized and space-saving installations. Each type caters to specific mounting requirements for different applications.</p></p>
