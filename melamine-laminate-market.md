<p><p>Melamine laminate is a decorative surface material commonly used for countertops, cabinets, furniture, and flooring. It is made by impregnating sheets of paper with melamine resin and then thermally fusing it to a substrate such as particleboard or plywood. Melamine laminate offers durability, scratch resistance, and a wide range of decorative options, making it a popular choice for both residential and commercial applications.</p><p>The melamine laminate market is expected to experience steady growth, with a projected CAGR of 4.9% during the forecast period. Overall, the market is expected to continue its upward trajectory due to the versatility and cost-effectiveness of melamine laminate in various applications.</p></p>
