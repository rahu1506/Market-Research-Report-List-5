<p><p>The calcined anthracite market consists of two primary types: electrically calcined and gas calcined anthracite. Electrically calcined anthracite is produced using electrical heat, which allows for high purity and enhanced performance in applications like electrodes and lubricants. In contrast, gas calcined anthracite is processed using gas as a heat source, often resulting in a more economical product suitable for various industrial uses, including as a carbon additive in steel production. Both types are essential for diverse industries.</p></p>
