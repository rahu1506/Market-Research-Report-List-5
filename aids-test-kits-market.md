<p><strong>The AIDS Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody Tests</li><li>RNA (viral load) Test</li><li>Antibody-antigen Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AIDS test kits are available in three main types - antibody tests, RNA (viral load) tests, and antibody-antigen tests. Antibody tests detect the presence of HIV antibodies in the blood, indicating past or current infection. RNA tests measure the amount of HIV genetic material in the blood, providing information on how active the virus is in the body. Antibody-antigen tests detect both HIV antibodies and antigens, offering a quicker detection of the virus compared to traditional antibody tests.</p></p>
