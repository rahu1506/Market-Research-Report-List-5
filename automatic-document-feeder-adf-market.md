<p><strong>The Automatic Document Feeder (ADF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simplex (One Side Scanning)</li><li>Duplex (Two Side Scanning)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Document Feeder (ADF) market includes two types: Simplex and Duplex. Simplex ADFs scan documents one side at a time, making them suitable for basic scanning needs. On the other hand, Duplex ADFs can scan both sides of a document simultaneously, increasing efficiency and saving time. Duplex ADFs are ideal for handling larger volumes of documents and are commonly used in offices where quick and accurate scanning is required. Both types cater to different scanning requirements based on the level of functionality needed.</p></p>
