<p><p>Elliptical trainers are available in three main types based on their drive position: front drive, rear drive, and center drive. Front drive ellipticals have the flywheel positioned at the front, providing a more compact design and a natural walking motion. Rear drive ellipticals have the flywheel positioned at the back, offering a smoother and quieter workout experience. Center drive ellipticals have the flywheel positioned in the middle for a more balanced and stable feel during workouts. Each type caters to different preferences and workout styles.</p></p>
