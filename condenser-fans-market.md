<p><p>Condenser fans are divided into two main types based on their power supply: single phase and three phase condenser fans. Single phase condenser fans are powered by a single alternating current, making them ideal for residential and smaller commercial applications. On the other hand, three phase condenser fans are powered by three alternating currents, providing more power and efficiency, making them suitable for larger commercial and industrial settings. Both types of condenser fans play a crucial role in dissipating heat from refrigeration systems to maintain optimal performance.</p></p>
