<p><p>High Temperature Vacuum Sintering Furnace market can be divided into two types based on the method of reclaiming material - Unilateral Reclaiming Method and Bilateral Reclaiming Method. In the Unilateral Reclaiming Method, materials are loaded and unloaded from one side of the furnace, while in the Bilateral Reclaiming Method, materials are loaded and unloaded from both sides of the furnace.
