<p><p>Corticosteroid-responsive dermatoses are skin conditions that can be treated with corticosteroids. There are two main types of corticosteroids - glucocorticoids and mineralocorticoids - that are commonly used in the treatment of these skin diseases. Glucocorticoids primarily have anti-inflammatory effects and are effective in reducing inflammation and itching associated with dermatoses. Mineralocorticoids, on the other hand, help regulate salt and water balance in the body. Both types of corticosteroids play a crucial role in managing corticosteroid-responsive dermatoses.</p></p>
