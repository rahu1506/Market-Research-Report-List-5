Tenor mandolins are larger still and provide deeper tones, followed by bass mandolins with even lower frequencies. Contrabass mandolins are the largest and lowest-pitched, delivering rich, resonant tones. Each market type caters to different musical styles and preferences, offering a wide range of options for musicians to choose from.</p></p>
