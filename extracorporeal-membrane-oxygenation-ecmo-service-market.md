<p><p>Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) services market can be divided into two main types: Veno-Arterial (VA) and Veno-Venous (VV) ECMO services. VA ECMO provides both cardiac and respiratory support by removing oxygen-depleted blood from the venous system, oxygenating it, and returning it to the arterial system. VV ECMO solely supports respiratory function by removing oxygen-depleted blood from the venous system, oxygenating it, and returning it to the venous system. These two types of ECMO services cater to different patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
