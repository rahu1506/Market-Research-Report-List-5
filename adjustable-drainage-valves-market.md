<p><ul><li>Magnetic Adjustment</li><li>Gravity Adjustment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adjustable drainage valves come in two main types: magnetic adjustment and gravity adjustment. Magnetic adjustment valves use magnets to control the flow of water, allowing for precise adjustments to be made easily. On the other hand, gravity adjustment valves rely on gravity to control the flow rate, with changes made by altering the position of the valve. Both types offer efficient drainage solutions for various applications, but magnetic adjustment valves provide more precise control while gravity adjustment valves are simpler in design and operation.</p></p>
