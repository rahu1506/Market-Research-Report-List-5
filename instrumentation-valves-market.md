<p><p>Instrumentation valves market is divided into various types based on their functionality and design. Ball valves are used for on/off control of flow, while needle valves provide precise flow control. Check valves prevent backflow in a system, while manifold valves help in connecting multiple instruments to a single source. Ultraclean valves are designed for applications requiring high purity levels. Other valves in the market include butterfly valves, diaphragm valves, and pinch valves, each serving a specific purpose in fluid control systems.</p></p>
