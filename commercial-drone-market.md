<p><p>There are four main types of commercial drone markets: fixed-wing, rotary blade, nano, and hybrid. Fixed-wing drones are typically used for long-distance missions and can cover large areas efficiently. Rotary blade drones are versatile and can hover, take off and land vertically. Nano drones are small and lightweight, suitable for indoor and close-quarters missions. Hybrid drones combine the benefits of fixed-wing and rotary blade drones, offering flexibility in mission capabilities. Each type serves a specific purpose in the commercial drone industry.</p></p>
