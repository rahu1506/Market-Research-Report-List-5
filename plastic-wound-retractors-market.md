<p><p>Plastic wound retractors are medical devices used during surgeries to hold back incisions and provide a clear view of the surgical site. There are two main types of plastic wound retractors: ring-based and prong-based. Ring-based retractors have a circular ring that attaches to the skin surrounding the incision, while prong-based retractors have multiple pointed prongs that hold back the wound edges. Both types provide adequate exposure for surgeons to perform procedures effectively and safely.</p></p>
