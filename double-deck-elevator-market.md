<p><strong>The Double Deck Elevator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traction</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Double Deck Elevator Market consists of two main types: Traction and Hydraulic. Traction double deck elevators use steel cables and a counterweight to raise and lower the elevator cab. They are known for their high efficiency and speed. Hydraulic double deck elevators, on the other hand, use a hydraulic piston to move the cab. They are generally slower but more cost-effective and suitable for low-rise buildings. Both types offer unique advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the building and users.</p></p>
