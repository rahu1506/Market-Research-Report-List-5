<p><p>Ambulatory cardiac monitoring equipment includes various types such as Holter monitor, event monitors, mobile cardiac telemetry, and other emerging markets. Holter monitors are wearable devices that continuously record heart activity for 24-48 hours. Event monitors are used intermittently to record the heart's activity during symptoms. Mobile cardiac telemetry devices are wearable monitors that transmit real-time data to healthcare providers. Other emerging markets may include implantable loop recorders or patch monitors for long-term monitoring of cardiac rhythms.</p></p>
