<p><p>Sclerotherapy is a medical procedure used to treat varicose veins and spider veins. There are three main types of sclerosing agents used in the market - Detergents, Osmotic Agents, and Chemical Irritants. Detergents work by breaking down the cells lining the vein walls. Osmotic agents create a hypertonic environment that causes the veins to shrink. Chemical irritants cause inflammation and scarring in the vein, leading to its closure. Each type of sclerosing agent has its own advantages and limitations in treating varicose veins.</p></p>
