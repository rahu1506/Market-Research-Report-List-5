Single Channel systems process video from one source, while Four Channel systems can handle inputs from up to four sources simultaneously. Eight Channel systems can process video from up to eight sources at once, offering greater capacity. Systems categorized as Others may support a different number of channels, offering flexibility to match the specific needs of users.</p></p>
