<p><p>Angiographic interventional therapy systems are used for diagnosing and treating conditions related to blood vessels. There are four main types of angiography markets: X-ray angiography, CT angiography, Magnetic Resonance Angiography, and MR-CT angiography. X-ray angiography uses X-rays to create detailed images of blood vessels. CT angiography utilizes computed tomography technology for imaging blood vessels. Magnetic Resonance Angiography employs magnetic resonance imaging for visualizing blood vessels. MR-CT angiography combines both MRI and CT technologies for more accurate imaging of blood vessels.</p></p>
