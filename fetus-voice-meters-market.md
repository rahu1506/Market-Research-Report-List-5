<p><p> </p><p>Doppler fetus-voice meters are devices that use ultrasound technology to detect and amplify the sound of the fetus's heartbeat. Both types of meters are used by healthcare professionals to monitor the health and development of the fetus during pregnancy. The market for these devices varies based on their technology and features, with Doppler meters typically being more advanced and expensive than passive meters.</p></p>
