<p><p>DC Tachogenerators are used to measure rotational speed in machines. There are two main types in the market: Permanent Magnet Type and Electromagnetic Type. Permanent Magnet Type tachogenerators use a permanent magnet to generate a voltage output proportional to the speed of the shaft rotation. Electromagnetic Type tachogenerators use an electromagnet to generate a voltage output based on the speed of the shaft rotation. Both types are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace for speed control applications.</p></p>
