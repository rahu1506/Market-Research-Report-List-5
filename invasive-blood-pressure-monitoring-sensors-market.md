<p><p>Invasive blood pressure monitoring sensors come in three main types: Single Channel Sensor, Dual Channel Sensor, and Triple Channel Sensor. Single Channel Sensors measure only one pressure parameter, while Dual Channel Sensors measure two parameters simultaneously. Triple Channel Sensors can measure three parameters at once. Each of these sensors provides healthcare professionals with real-time data on a patient's blood pressure, helping them make accurate and timely treatment decisions. The variety of options allows for customization based on the specific needs of the patient and healthcare setting.</p></p>
