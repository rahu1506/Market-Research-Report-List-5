<p><p>The DisplayPort market includes various types catering to diverse applications. Embedded DisplayPort (eDP) is used in laptops and monitors for internal connections. Wireless DisplayPort facilitates cable-free connections for mobile devices. Internal DisplayPort connects components within devices, while Micro DisplayPort is a compact version for portable devices. MyDP/SlimPort allows HDMI and DisplayPort features via mobile devices. Mini DisplayPort, favored by Apple and others, provides a smaller connector option, enhancing versatility across a range of devices and setups.</p></p>
