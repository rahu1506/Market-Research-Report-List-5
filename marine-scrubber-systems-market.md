<p><p>Marine scrubber systems are used to reduce emissions from ships. There are different types of scrubbers in the market including open loop scrubbers, closed loop scrubbers, hybrid scrubbers, membrane scrubbers, and dry scrubbers. Open loop scrubbers use seawater, closed loop scrubbers use circulating water, hybrid scrubbers can switch between open and closed loop modes, membrane scrubbers use a polymer membrane, and dry scrubbers use a dry sorbent material. Each type of scrubber has unique features and benefits for reducing pollutants in marine exhaust emissions.</p></p>
