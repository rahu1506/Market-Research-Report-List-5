<p><ul><li>Round Riveted Screw</li><li>Hexagon Head Riveted Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure riveting screws market includes two main types: round riveted screws and hexagon head riveted screws. Round riveted screws have a flat, circular head that provides a smooth appearance and even distribution of pressure. They are commonly used in applications where a flush finish is desired. On the other hand, hexagon head riveted screws have a hexagonal head shape which allows for easy installation and removal using a wrench or socket. They are often preferred for applications where higher torque is required.</p></p>
