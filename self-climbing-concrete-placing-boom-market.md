<p><ul><li>Climbing Inside Floors</li><li>Elevator Climb</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-climbing concrete placing boom market includes two main types: climbing inside floors and elevator climb. Climbing inside floors involves the placing boom being positioned inside the building and climbing up as the concrete is poured on each floor. Elevator climb, on the other hand, allows the placing boom to be attached to an elevator that moves vertically as the concrete is placed, providing flexibility and efficiency in high-rise construction projects. Both methods offer innovative solutions for reaching high heights and navigating tight spaces during concrete placement.</p></p>
