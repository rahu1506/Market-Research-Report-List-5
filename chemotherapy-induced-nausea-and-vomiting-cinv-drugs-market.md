<p><p>Chemotherapy Induced Nausea and Vomiting (CINV) Drugs market includes two main types of drugs: 5-HT3 Inhibitors and NK1 Inhibitors. 5-HT3 Inhibitors work by blocking serotonin receptors in the brain, helping to prevent nausea and vomiting. NK1 Inhibitors, on the other hand, target substance P, a neurotransmitter that triggers nausea and vomiting. Both types of drugs are commonly used in cancer patients undergoing chemotherapy to manage CINV and improve their quality of life.</p></p>
