<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Photonic IR Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid Wave Infrared</li><li>Long Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photonic IR sensors operate in three main market types based on the wavelength range they detect: Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR sensors typically detect wavelengths from 0.7 to 3 micrometers, ideal for applications like imaging through fog or smoke. MWIR sensors operate in the 3 to 5 micrometer range, commonly used for thermal imaging and surveillance. LWIR sensors cover wavelengths from 8 to 14 micrometers, suited for detecting heat signatures and thermal mapping.</p></p>
