<p><p>Multistage mixers are commonly found in two main markets: roller and canister. Roller mixers use a rotating drum or cylinder to mix materials together, making them ideal for mixing powders and granules. On the other hand, canister mixers involve a container with internal blades that spin and mix the ingredients. Canister mixers are popular for mixing liquids and semi-solids. Both types of mixers are used in various industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals for efficiently mixing materials in different stages.</p></p>
