<p><p>Multilayer high frequency inductors are available in two main types: SMD (surface mount device) and plug-in. SMD type inductors are designed for direct placement onto circuit boards, making them compact and suitable for automated assembly processes. On the other hand, plug-in type inductors are larger and require manual insertion into a circuit board. Both types cater to different market needs, with SMD inductors being more popular for high-volume electronic devices and plug-in inductors commonly used in specialized applications requiring higher power handling capabilities.</p></p>
