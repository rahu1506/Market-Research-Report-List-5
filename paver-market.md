<p><ul><li>Mechanical Pavers</li><li>Hydrostatic Pavers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of pavers in the market: Mechanical Pavers and Hydrostatic Pavers. Mechanical pavers are suitable for high-speed paving with adjustable paving widths and depths. They are commonly used for large-scale construction projects. Hydrostatic pavers, on the other hand, provide superior paving efficiency and precision due to their hydraulic systems that control speed and power distribution. They are more commonly used in smaller, more intricate paving jobs. Both types of pavers offer unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the project.</p></p>
