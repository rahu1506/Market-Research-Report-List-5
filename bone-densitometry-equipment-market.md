<p><p>Bone densitometry equipment market includes two main types - Ultrasound Bone Densitometer and X-ray Bone Densitometer. Ultrasound Bone Densitometer uses sound waves to measure bone density, making it a portable and radiation-free option suitable for screening purposes. On the other hand, X-ray Bone Densitometer uses X-ray technology to assess bone density and is considered more accurate for diagnosing osteoporosis. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in evaluating bone health and helping healthcare professionals in diagnosing and monitoring bone diseases.</p></p>
