<p><p>Carrot seed oil is available in two main market types: natural and synthetic. Natural carrot seed oil is extracted from the seeds of the Daucus carota plant through steam distillation, retaining its natural properties and benefits. On the other hand, synthetic carrot seed oil is produced in a laboratory using artificial substances to mimic the aroma and properties of the natural oil. While natural carrot seed oil is preferred for its authenticity and purity, synthetic options are often cheaper and more readily available.</p></p>
