<p><strong>The IGBT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IGBT Discretes</li><li>IGBT Modules</li><li>IGBT-IPM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IGBT market consists of three main types: IGBT Discretes, IGBT Modules, and IGBT-IPM. IGBT Discretes are individual IGBT transistors that are commonly used for small-scale applications. IGBT Modules combine multiple IGBTs, diodes, and other components into a single package for higher power applications. IGBT-IPM, or Intelligent Power Modules, integrate IGBTs with gate drivers and protection circuits for easier implementation in motor control and renewable energy systems. Each type provides different levels of integration and performance for various industrial applications.</p></p>
