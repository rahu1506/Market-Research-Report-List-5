<p><p>Pharmaceutical vial rubber stoppers come in two main types - conventional stoppers and film-coated stoppers. Conventional stoppers are the traditional type and are made of pure rubber, providing a reliable seal for the vials. On the other hand, film-coated stoppers have an additional layer of film coating, which offers enhanced chemical resistance and can prevent drug interactions. Both types of stoppers play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and safety of pharmaceutical products stored in vials.</p></p>
