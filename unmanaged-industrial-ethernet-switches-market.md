<p><p>Unmanaged Industrial Ethernet Switches come in three main market types based on the type of ports they use: RJ-45 Ethernet Switches, BNC Ethernet Switches, and AUI Ethernet Switches. RJ-45 Ethernet Switches are the most common type and use standard RJ-45 connectors for connecting Ethernet cables. BNC Ethernet Switches use BNC connectors, which are commonly used in older networking equipment. AUI Ethernet Switches use AUI ports, which are a type of connector used for connecting devices in a local area network. Each of these switch types caters to different connectivity needs in industrial settings.</p></p>
