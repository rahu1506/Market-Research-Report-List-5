<p><strong>The Inflight Internet System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ATG</li><li>Ka Band Satellite</li><li>Ku Band Satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inflight Internet System market includes three main types: ATG (Air-to-Ground) technology uses ground-based cell towers to provide connectivity to aircraft while in flight. Ka Band Satellite technology utilizes high-frequency satellites to deliver internet services to airplanes. Ku Band Satellite technology also uses satellites to provide connectivity, operating on a lower frequency than Ka Band. Each of these market types offers different advantages in terms of coverage, speed, and reliability for airlines seeking to offer inflight Wi-Fi to passengers.</p></p>
