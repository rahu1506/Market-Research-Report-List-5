<p><p>Front Side Silver Paste and Back Side Silver Paste are two types of silver pastes used in solar cells. Front Side Silver Paste is applied to the front surface of the solar cell to create electrical contacts and collect the generated electricity. Back Side Silver Paste is applied to the back surface to improve reflectivity and prevent the recombination of electrons and holes. Both types of silver pastes play crucial roles in enhancing the efficiency and performance of solar cells.</p></p>
