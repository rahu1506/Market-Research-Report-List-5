<p><p>Mask tester market comprises of three main types: </p><p>1. Fit Tester: These testers evaluate how well a mask fits on an individual's face to ensure proper protection against contaminants. They are crucial in determining the effectiveness of a mask in preventing leakage.</p><p>2. Integrity Tester: This type of tester checks for any damages or defects in the mask material that could compromise its integrity and effectiveness in filtering out harmful particles.</p><p>3. Performance Tester: These testers assess the overall performance of the mask by measuring factors such as filtration efficiency, breathability, and pressure drop to ensure it meets safety standards.</p></p>
